Notes on ITRANS
The Itrans Transliteration Scheme is possibly the most popular and easy Roman-Devanagari Encoding Scheme ever devised. It is authored by Avinash Chopde and widely used on the Web( details available on ). It is extremely easy to use and memorize due to its phonetic character.
Also consult http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ITRANS
