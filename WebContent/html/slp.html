Notes on SLP:
SLP Transliteration Scheme is a one-to-one Roman-Devanagari Encoding Scheme devised by Prof. Peter M. Scharf and used at his site http://sanskritlibrary.org.
Its main advantage lies in it's 'One Roman Equivalent for One Devanagari Symbol' approach.
